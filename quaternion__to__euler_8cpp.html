Euler321 MathUtils::quaternion_to_euler ( const Quaternion &  q)

Convert a quaternion to 321 Euler angles.

No pitch singularity checks. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Conversion_between_quaternions_and_Euler_angles#Quaternion_to_Euler_angles_(in_3-2-1_sequence)_conversion

Parameters
qQuaternion to convert.
Returns
Quaternion converted to 321 Euler angles [rad].

No pitch singularity (close to +/- 90 deg) checks performed here. Note that -asin(2.0 * ((q1*q3) - (q0*q2))) = asin(2.0 * ((q0*q2) - (q1*q3))) Below assumes unit quaternion ||q|| = 1
